RULES *
Hello! Welcome to Numeric Ninza! A game where you have to predict a number from 0 to 100. There are some basic rules of this game that make it more interesting.

1. ₹150 is given to all the users, which allows them to predict.

2. In your first five attempts For every wrong prediction, ₹10 will be debited from your wallet, and ₹40 will be credited for a correct prediction.

3. After five unsuccessful attempts, ₹20 will be debited from your wallet, and only ₹30 will be credited to your wallet for a correct prediction.

4. You can't continue your prediction if your wallet has an insufficient amount.

5. Every time you click on RESTART button, a new number will be generated. So, that's all about the game, enjoy it, 'Thank you...
